Conclusion

The study showed that all three personalities studied – Pieter Mouthaan, Michael Moore and Kenneth Dean – raise serious suspicions of authenticity. The analysis of the digital footprint, verification of professional data and images revealed significant inconsistencies that indicate possible fraud or fictitiousness of these persons.

1. Pieter Mouthaan
The study did not confirm the existence of a person with that name, which corresponds to the stated data. Although there is an AI project called Deep Agency, developed by a man from the Netherlands, its connection with Pieter Mouthaan has not been established. In addition, the lack of a digital footprint, professional mentions, and academic background makes his identity highly questionable.

Conclusion: Most likely, Pieter Mouthaan is a fictional character created to mislead.

2. Michael Moore
Checking the name and the stated facts about the business, family and place of residence did not provide any confirmation. Moreover, the results of a reverse image search showed that the photos supposedly belonging to Michael Moore actually belong to another person. This indicates the possible use of stolen images to create a fake profile.

Conclusion: Michael Moore's identity is most likely fictitious.

3. Kenneth Dean
Despite the presence of Kenneth Dean's LinkedIn profile and the mention of his name on the company's website, the lack of photographs, business activities and public records of his professional activities raises serious doubts. Another alarming signal is the minimal number of subscribers and interactions on LinkedIn, which is atypical for a company vice president. A reverse image search also revealed discrepancies with the specified personality.

Conclusion: The probability that Kenneth Dean is a fake profile is extremely high.

The overall result
All three personalities show signs of fakes or fraudulent schemes:
  • Lack of a digital footprint in the professional environment.
  • The discrepancy between the provided images and the real data.
  • False or unconfirmed biographical details.

Based on these factors, it can be concluded that Pieter Mouthaan, Michael Moore and Kenneth Dean are highly likely to be fictional personalities. It is recommended to exercise caution when interacting with such profiles and to conduct a thorough check before trusting the online identity.
