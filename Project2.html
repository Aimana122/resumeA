What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small pieces of text stored in your browser to remember information about your visit. Websites use them to:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that expire when you close the browser.
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain after the browser is closed.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by other domains (ad networks, analytics, etc).
  • Secure Cookies: Only transmitted over HTTPS.
  • HttpOnly Cookies: Can't be accessed with JavaScript (for sensitive info).
